Welcome to the Virtual Etch-a-Sketch! This little square has unlimited drawing potential -- you can literally make a earth-shattering, culture-influencing career out of your little pixel art. Well, maybe not...but this is a cool little thing to waste 5 minutes on the toilet. Anyways, here are the instructions: Drag your mouse inside the red square (or tap your finger if you're on a touch device) and draw away! If you mess up, you can clear the board simply by clicking New Sketch. To adjust sketch settings, hover or click the options link at the top right. In the drop down menu you will see a block density slider, an opacity checkbox, and a download sketch button. All are pretty self-explanatory. However, to set the density, adjust the slider then click the blue New Sketch button at the top left. To download your sketch, make a sketch then click Download Sketch, and your .jpg should appear momentarily. Enjoy!
